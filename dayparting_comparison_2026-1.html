✔ Verdict: Does the theory hold in 2026?

Yes — the quality signal is clear and consistent. Players acquired during the High Disposable Income Period (23rd–30th) show meaningfully better long-term metrics across every quality indicator: PLTV/PP is +13.8% higher, Avg FTD Value is +6.8% higher, and crucially the 2-deposit retention rate is +33% higher (25.1% vs 18.8%), which is arguably the strongest behavioural signal for predicted lifetime value.

The Low Period (10th–22nd) wins only on raw volume — which makes sense given it covers nearly double the calendar days and benefits from major sporting events (Cheltenham, etc.). Per unique visitor, the High Period is more efficient: 575 uniques/day vs 484/day, converting at better quality. The SUO cost being 33% cheaper (as of GP) in the High Period also supports increasing bids/budgets during those windows.
